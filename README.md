# Week 1 Day 1: The Profile Printer

Welcome to your first coding assignment! 

### The Mission
You need to print an "Employee Badge" to the console. This will test your ability to use `System.out.println` and special formatting characters (Escape Sequences).

### Requirements
- Your badge must be enclosed in a box of `*` or `-`.
- You must include your **Name**, **Role**, and **Department**.
- You must use `\t` to align your text inside the box.
- The program must run without errors.

### Success Criteria
When you push your code, the auto-grader will look for:
1. The words "NAME", "ROLE", and "DEPT".
2. A border made of asterisks or dashes.
3. Successful compilation.
